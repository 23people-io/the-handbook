## About Us

### Who We Are (docs/about-us/who-we-are/index.md)

**23people is a technology company founded on November 26, 2014, by software engineers Marcelo Ampuero and Manuel Reyes.** The founding principle was to create a company where they themselves would want to workâ€”a place that prioritizes people, fosters a passion for technology, and promotes continuous learning. We are a team of professionals passionate about what we do, aiming to build a sustainable business model that puts people first in our relationships with both clients and our team.

The name **"23people"** is inspired by the movie *The Matrix Reloaded*, referencing the 23 individuals selected to rebuild Zion. It represents the spirit behind those chosen to build something new outside the conventional system.

### What We Do (docs/about-us/what-we-do/services.md)

**We are trusted technology facilitators and co-creators for companies looking to evolve their digital products and services.** We help clients navigate their Digital Evolution by integrating Tech Talent, Software Engineering, and advanced technologies with their business knowledge and data potential.

Our services are structured into three main lines:

1.  **Tech Talent Outsourcing and Recruitment**: We specialize in finding, vetting, and retaining high-level Tech Professionals and Teams. We offer **IT Outsourcing**, where professionals work on client projects under our legal structure, and **IT Hunting** for direct client hires.
2.  **Digital Platforms, Cloud, and Modernization**: We co-create and evolve effective, secure, and efficient digital platforms using Cloud, DevOps, QA Automation, and Agile practices. This includes **Integration**, **Modernization & Evolution**, **Data & Analytics Infrastructure**, and **Automated QA**.
3.  **Data, Advanced Analytics & AI**: We enable data-driven decision-making by combining Big Data technologies, predictive and generative AI tools (Machine Learning, RAG, LLMs), and advanced automation (AI Agents) to generate real value in daily operations.

### What Sets Us Apart (docs/about-us/how-we-work/index.md)

Our differentiation lies in *how* we do things, based on five fundamental pillars:

1.  **Specialization in Tech Roles**: We focus on finding exceptional and high-potential Software Programmers and Engineers who are passionate about their craft.
2.  **Optimization in Tech Recruitment**: We have a mature, proven process for finding and selecting top tech talent.
3.  **Maturity in Software Development**: We build enterprise software in an agile, evolutionary, and data-driven manner.
4.  **Mastery of Enterprise and Emerging Technologies**: We have validated experience in a wide range of mature and emerging technologies, which we use to build software and validate professional skills.
5.  **Client Satisfaction**: We are committed to helping and supporting our clients throughout every project, ensuring they feel guided and accompanied.

### What We Pursue (docs/about-us/what-we-pursue/goals.md)

Our strategy is guided by ambitious long-term goals and foundational principles. We aim to:

1.  **Become a Benchmark in Technology and Tech Talent Growth**: To be recognized as leaders in high-performance IT teams, world-class digital platforms, and the use of AI tools in the LATAM market.
2.  **Achieve Sustainable Economic Growth and Globalization**: To grow our economic footprint sustainably while being a reference for professional development in IT, achieving high retention and satisfaction.
3.  **Innovate with Digital Products based on Data and AI**: To create new digital products and services through an AI-native culture, where artificial intelligence enhances our human capabilities to build effective and efficient solutions.

Our strategic execution is guided by the principle that **execution matters most**. We use frameworks like **OKRs** to align our teams and materialize our goals systematically.

## Organization

### Overview (docs/organization/index.md)

23people is organized in a **circular structure** of multidisciplinary teams to foster collaboration and communication. Each team operates with a clear purpose and delivers services to other internal teams or external clients through well-defined **service contracts (APIs)**. This model promotes autonomy while ensuring alignment with the company's strategic goals.

We distinguish between **Positions (Cargos)**, which define a professional's capabilities and level within the organization, and **Roles**, which are specific responsibilities assumed within a team or project. This flexibility allows professionals to apply their skills across different contexts.

### Teams (docs/organization/index.md)

Our operational teams are grouped under the Iarvis Tribe and are designed to function autonomously, guided by data-driven iterative improvement.

*   **Tech Talent Services (TTS)**: Delivers the best experience in the selection, validation, and retention of Tech Professionals who match our clients' project needs.
*   **Sales & Marketing (SMKT)**: Responsible for acquiring new clients, managing current commercial relationships, and growing the 23people brand and market presence.
*   **Backoffice (BKO)**: Provides agile and efficient administrative support in People Management, Financial Management, and Company Management, enabling other teams to focus on their core functions.
*   **Research & Development (R&D)**: Drives the digital evolution of our clients by exploring, mastering, and applying emerging technologies to generate real business value.

### Tribes (docs/organization/tribes/index.md)

Teams are grouped into two **Tribes** that share common objectives:

*   **Iarvis Tribe**: Comprises all of 23people's internal operational teams (TTS, SMKT, BKO, R&D). Its purpose is to drive the delivery of high-quality, sustainable services aligned with strategic goals.
*   **Drakkar Tribe**: Includes all professionals and teams working in outsourcing arrangements for our clients, whether as dedicated teams or as individual contributors.

### Councils (docs/organization/index.md)

Strategic and operational alignment is facilitated through councils:

*   **High Council**: A strategic team responsible for defining and communicating the organization's strategy, ensuring all operational teams are aligned with it.
*   **Iarvis Council**: An operational body, led by the Global Operations Manager, that coordinates and aligns the Iarvis Tribe teams, translating strategic goals into operational initiatives.

### Workforce Structure (docs/about-us/how-we-work/tech-roles.md)

Our workforce is structured by professional seniority, which we measure by skill development and acquired learning, not by years of experience.

*   **Entry**: A professional starting their career, requiring guidance to complete tasks efficiently. They demonstrate a continuous learning capacity and proactively seek help.
*   **Intermediate**: A professional with proven experience who serves as a reference for others. They handle responsibilities confidently and are developing their ability to align technical solutions with business goals.
*   **Senior**: A professional with extensive experience and deep knowledge, acting as an undisputed reference in best practices. They can take on roles like Tech Lead, Team Lead, Specialist, or Architect, and are responsible for effort and resource estimations.

## Principles

These principles define our core identity and guide our actions.

*   **Excellence and Intellectual Curiosity**: We are lifelong learners who pursue excellence in everything we do. We have an open mind, embrace iterative and data-driven work methods, and share what we learn.

These principles shape our interactions and work environment.

*   **Agility**: We embrace agile principles to be innovative, resilient, and efficient. We value individuals and interactions, working software, customer collaboration, and responding to change.

*   **Sustainability**: We are committed to a long-term vision that ensures the responsible use of natural, financial, and social resources. We are a 100% digital and cloud-based company, privileging remote work and technologies that reduce our environmental impact.

## Practices

### Agile Management (docs/practices/agile-management/workflows-manage-work-not-people.md)

We **manage the work, not the people**. Our approach is centered on visual and systematic workflows using **Kanban** principles. This allows us to visualize work, limit work-in-progress (WIP) to maximize efficiency, and foster a culture of continuous improvement. We focus on how work gets done, promoting collaboration, autonomy, and team responsibility.

### Communication (docs/practices/communication/asynchronous-communication.md)

We prioritize **asynchronous communication** to allow collaborators to manage their time and energy effectively, reducing interruptions. We encourage writing down ideas before sharing them to foster clarity and reflection. Key practices include acknowledging messages promptly to reduce uncertainty and using a structured narrative (Context, Question, Answer) for presenting initiatives to ensure clarity.

### Learning (docs/practices/learning/how-to-learn.md)

We follow a technique for **learning how to learn**. This involves selecting a topic, explaining it simply as if teaching someone else, identifying knowledge gaps and restudying them, and simplifying the language until the concept is deeply understood. This method ensures robust and long-lasting comprehension.

### Using AI (docs/practices/using-ai/claude-project.md)

We use AI as a **professional capability multiplier**. We leverage tools like **Claude Projects** to create dedicated workspaces with predefined instructions and context. This allows our teams to automate repetitive tasks, maintain quality standards, and focus on higher-value activities. All AI-generated content is treated as a first draft that requires professional review and refinement.

## Technical Guide

### Software Development Principles (docs/technical-guide/software-development/principles.md)

Our development philosophy is rooted in simplifying real-world problems.

*   **Simplicity First**: We eliminate unnecessary complexity to find the most direct and efficient solution.
*   **Balanced Paradigm**: We blend the best of Object-Oriented Programming (modularity, abstraction) and Functional Programming (immutability, pure functions) for clean, testable code.
*   **SOLID Principles**: We adhere to SOLID principles (Single Responsibility, Open/Closed, Liskov Substitution, Interface Segregation, Dependency Inversion) to build flexible and maintainable software.
*   **Business-Centric Focus**: We deeply understand the business problem before writing code to ensure we deliver real value.

### Methodologies (docs/technical-guide/software-development/methodologies.md)

We use an agile approach based on the **Kanban** method. This provides flexibility and focuses on optimizing workflow. Key aspects include visualizing work on Kanban boards, using **Work-in-Progress (WIP) limits** to prevent overload, and practicing **continuous improvement** to identify and eliminate bottlenecks.

### Technologies (docs/about-us/how-we-work/stack.md)

We use a carefully selected, mature, and scalable technology stack. Our core technologies include **Node.js/NestJS** for backends, **React** for frontends, and **Python** for Machine Learning projects. For databases, we use **MongoDB** for flexibility and **PostgreSQL** for relational integrity. We also have expertise across a wide range of languages (Java, C#, Go), cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, Cloudflare), web frameworks (Next.js, Spring Boot, FastAPI), and AI tools (LlamaIndex, OpenAI, Anthropic).

### Practices (docs/technical-guide/software-development/practices.md)

We follow standardized practices to ensure quality and consistency.

*   **Version Control**: We use **Git** with structured workflows like GitFlow.
*   **Code Reviews**: All changes are peer-reviewed to improve quality and share knowledge.
*   **Automated Testing**: We maintain adequate test coverage (unit, integration, E2E) to ensure reliability.
*   **CI/CD**: We use continuous integration and delivery pipelines to automate builds, testing, and deployments.
*   **Security**: We integrate security tools like **SonarQube** for static analysis and **OWASP ZAP** for dynamic testing into our CI/CD pipeline to identify vulnerabilities early.

### Specializations (docs/technical-guide/software-development/ai-and-machine-learning.md)

*   **AI and Machine Learning**: We follow a full project lifecycle from problem identification to deployment and monitoring, using **Python** and its ecosystem. We adhere to **MLOps** practices for automation and ensure ethical considerations are addressed.
*   **Cloud**: We leverage cloud services for scalability and simplicity, prioritizing providers with strong sustainability commitments like **Cloudflare**, **AWS**, and **DigitalOcean**.
*   **Security**: Security is a priority. We use static analysis (SonarQube), dynamic analysis (OWASP ZAP), and linters (ESLint, RUFF) integrated into our CI/CD pipelines to ensure code quality and security.

## Sustainability

### Our Commitment (docs/sustainability/our-commitment.md)

We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint in line with climate science and the Paris Agreement. As a 100% digital and remote company, we adopt **Science Based Targets (SBTi)** to guide our efforts. Our goals include significant reductions in Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions and achieving carbon neutrality.

### Strategy (docs/sustainability/our-commitment.md)

Our strategy focuses on three key areas:
1.  **Energy and Technology Use**: Prioritizing cloud providers that use renewable energy and promoting energy-efficient hardware.
2.  **Reduction of Indirect Emissions (Scope 3)**: Minimizing business travel, promoting the longevity of electronic devices, and selecting sustainable suppliers.
3.  **Measurement and Reporting**: Annually measuring our carbon footprint and transparently reporting our progress.

### Team (docs/sustainability/team.md)

Sustainability is a cross-functional effort led by a dedicated team comprising the Global Operations Manager, a representative from People Growth, and a representative from the founders. This team is responsible for designing strategy, measuring impact, and acting as an agent of change within the organization.

### Technology Recommendations (docs/sustainability/technology-recommendations.md)

We provide clear guidelines for making sustainable technology choices. This includes prioritizing hardware from sustainable brands, using cloud services powered by renewable energy, and optimizing our digital infrastructure to minimize environmental impact.
