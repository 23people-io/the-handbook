## About Us

### What We Do (docs/about-us/what-we-do/index.md)

We act as **reliable technology facilitators and co-creators** for companies navigating their Digital Evolution. We help clients integrate Tech Talent, Software Engineering, and advanced technologies with their business knowledge and data potential. Our services are designed to address key business challenges such as justifying ROI on tech investments, finding a trustworthy technology partner, and sourcing validated tech professionals.

Our service lines include:

1.  **Outsourcing and Recruitment of Tech Talent**: We specialize in finding, selecting, and retaining high-level Tech Professionals and Teams. We offer both **IT Outsourcing** (professionals work under our legal entity) and **IT Hunting** (we assist clients in direct hiring).
2.  **Digital Platforms, Cloud, and Modernization**: We co-create and evolve digital platforms using Cloud, DevOps, and agile practices. This includes services for **Integration**, **Modernization**, **Data & Analytics**, and **Automated QA**.
3.  **Data, Advanced Analytics & AI**: We enable data-driven decision-making by combining Big Data technologies, predictive and generative AI tools (like Machine Learning and LLMs), and advanced automation to generate real operational value.

## Organization

### Overview (docs/organization/index.md)

23people is organized into **multidisciplinary teams** with a circular structure to foster collaboration. Each team operates with a clear purpose and autonomy, delivering services to internal teams or external clients through well-defined service contracts, much like an **API**. Key operational principles for teams include having a clear purpose, autonomy, fulfilling purpose through service contracts, and data-driven iterative improvement.

### Teams (docs/organization/index.md)

Our operational teams are grouped within the Iarvis Tribe and are led by a **Global Operations Manager**.

*   **Tech Talent Services (TTS)**: Delivers the best experience in the selection, validation, and retention of Tech Professionals who are passionate about technology and are the best fit for our clients' projects.
*   **Sales & Marketing (SMKT)**: Responsible for acquiring new clients, managing current commercial relationships, and growing the 23people brand and market presence.
*   **Backoffice (BKO)**: Provides agile and efficient administrative support in People Management, Financial Management, and Company Management, enabling other teams to focus on their core functions.
*   **Research & Development (R&D)**: Drives clients' digital evolution by exploring and mastering emerging technologies that create real business value.

### Tribes (docs/organization/tribes/index.md)

Teams are grouped into two **Tribes** that share common objectives:

*   **Iarvis Tribe**: Comprises all of 23people's internal operational teams (TTS, SMKT, BKO, R&D). Its purpose is to drive the delivery of high-quality, sustainable services aligned with strategic goals.
*   **Drakkar Tribe**: Includes all teams and professionals working on a subcontracting basis for client projects. Its purpose is to ensure we have the right talent to meet client needs.

### Councils (docs/organization/index.md)

*   **High Council**: A strategic team responsible for defining and communicating the organization's strategy and ensuring operational teams are aligned with it.
*   **Iarvis Council**: Responsible for the coordination and alignment of the operational teams within the Iarvis Tribe.

### Workforce Structure (docs/organization/index.md)

We distinguish between **Positions (Cargos)** and **Roles**.
*   A **Position** defines a professional's level and core competencies within the organization's hierarchy.
*   A **Role** defines a specific set of responsibilities a professional undertakes in a particular context, such as a team or project.

This flexible structure allows a professional in a single position (e.g., Senior Software Engineer) to perform different roles (e.g., Tech Lead, Technical Interviewer) across various teams. Key operational roles include:

*   **Global Operations Manager**: Orchestrates the operational execution of the Iarvis Tribe teams.
*   **Service Request Manager (SRM)**: Manages the "what" and "when" of service requests, acting as the primary interface with the client.
*   **Service Delivery Manager (SDM)**: Defines the "how" and "who" for service execution, ensuring quality and efficiency in delivery.

## Practices

### Agile Management (docs/practices/agile-management/workflows-manage-work-not-people.md)

We manage **work, not people**, using **Kanban** principles to visualize workflows and maximize efficiency. Our process includes:
*   **Visual Workflows**: Using Kanban boards with distinct areas (Backlog, Requested, In Progress, Done, Archive).
*   **WIP Limits**: Limiting work-in-progress to prevent overload and encourage focus on finishing tasks.
*   **Daily Meetings**: A structured daily sync to review board progress from right to left (Done to Requested), manage blockages, and define the day's focus.
*   **Documenting Decisions**: We prefer to document decisions rather than leaving them in people's memory to ensure clarity and persistence.

### Communication (docs/practices/communication/index.md)

*   **Asynchronous-First**: We prefer asynchronous communication to allow collaborators to manage their time effectively and provide thoughtful responses.
*   **Write Then Communicate**: We encourage writing down ideas to clarify them before sharing, which improves the quality of communication.
*   **Structured Narrative**: We use frameworks like SCQA (Situation, Complication, Question, Answer) to structure proposals and initiatives for clear and effective communication.
*   **Acknowledge Promptly**: We acknowledge receipt of messages as soon as possible to reduce ambiguity and anxiety.

### Learning (docs/practices/learning/how-to-learn.md)

We follow a technique for effective learning:
1.  Select and study a topic.
2.  Explain it aloud as if teaching someone else to identify knowledge gaps.
3.  Return to studying when encountering difficulties.
4.  Simplify the language and use analogies.
5.  Review for clarity; if it's confusing, the topic isn't fully understood yet.

### Using AI (docs/practices/using-ai/claude-project.md)

We use AI, specifically **Claude Projects**, as a capability multiplier. This allows us to create dedicated workspaces with pre-defined instructions to automate repetitive tasks, maintain quality standards, and free up our teams to focus on high-value activities. The generated content is always treated as a draft that requires professional review and refinement.

## Technical Guide

### Software Development Principles (docs/technical-guide/software-development/principles.md)

Our development philosophy is centered on **simplifying real-world problems**.
*   **Simplicity Above All**: We strive for the simplest possible solution, eliminating unnecessary complexity.
*   **Balance of OOP and Functional Programming**: We leverage concepts from both paradigms to create modular, readable, and testable code.
*   **SOLID Principles**: We adhere to Single Responsibility, Open/Closed, Liskov Substitution, Interface Segregation, and Dependency Inversion principles.
*   **Business-Centric Approach**: We focus on understanding and solving the core business problem to deliver tangible value.
*   **Scalability and Maintainability**: We design solutions that are built to grow and evolve in an orderly fashion.

### Methodologies (docs/technical-guide/software-development/methodologies.md)

We use a **Kanban-based agile method** that emphasizes a continuous flow of work. This includes visualizing the workflow on boards, using WIP limits to prevent bottlenecks, and practicing continuous improvement to optimize our processes. Our methodology is flexible and can be adapted with practices from other frameworks as needed.

### Technologies (docs/technical-guide/software-development/technologies.md)

Our primary technology stack is chosen for its mature ecosystem, scalability, and flexibility:
*   **Backend**: **Node.js / NestJS**
*   **Frontend**: **React**
*   **AI/ML**: **Python** with its extensive data science libraries.
*   **Databases**: **MongoDB** (NoSQL) for flexibility and **PostgreSQL** (SQL) for robustness and ACID transactions.

We also have broad experience with numerous other languages (Java, C#, Go), cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, Cloudflare), web frameworks (Next.js, Spring Boot, FastAPI), and AI tools (LlamaIndex, OpenAI API).

### Practices (docs/technical-guide/software-development/practices.md)

We ensure quality and consistency through standardized practices:
*   **Version Control**: Using **Git** with structured branching workflows (e.g., GitFlow).
*   **Code Reviews**: Mandatory for all proposed changes to foster collaboration and quality.
*   **Automated Testing**: Maintaining adequate coverage at unit, integration, and end-to-end levels.
*   **CI/CD**: Automating build, test, and deployment pipelines.
*   **Security**: Using tools like **SonarQube** for static analysis, **Burp Suite/OWASP ZAP** for pentesting, and linters like **RUFF** (Python) and **ESLint** (JS/TS) to ensure code quality and security.
*   **Monitoring & Observability**: Implementing logging and monitoring to ensure application health in production.

### Specializations (docs/technical-guide/software-development/ai-and-machine-learning.md)

*   **AI and Machine Learning**: We manage the full ML project lifecycle, from problem identification to deployment and monitoring (MLOps), with a strong focus on ethics and responsibility.
*   **Cloud**: We leverage cloud services (primarily from Cloudflare, AWS, and DigitalOcean) to ensure scalability, availability, and simplicity, with a strong preference for providers committed to sustainability and low-emissions.

## Sustainability

### Our Commitment (sustainability/our-commitment.md)

23people is committed to reducing its carbon footprint in line with the **Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi)** and the Paris Agreement. As a fully digital and remote company, our goals include:
*   Reducing Scope 1 and 2 emissions by at least **50% by 2030**.
*   Achieving **carbon neutrality by 2040**.
*   Reducing Scope 3 emissions (e.g., business travel, personal equipment) by **30%**.

### Strategy (sustainability/our-commitment.md)

Our implementation strategy focuses on:
*   **Energy and Technology**: Prioritizing cloud providers with 100% renewable energy certifications and promoting energy-efficient hardware.
*   **Indirect Emissions**: Reducing business travel in favor of virtual meetings and selecting partners with aligned sustainability commitments.
*   **Measurement and Reporting**: Annually measuring our carbon footprint and transparently reporting our progress.

### Team (sustainability/team.md)

We have a dedicated **sustainability team**, led by the Global Operations Manager and including representatives from People Growth and the founders, to oversee the implementation and monitoring of our sustainability policies.

### Technology Recommendations (docs/principles/sustainability.md)

We promote sustainability through technology choices such as:
*   **Remote Work**: Reducing commuting and office-related emissions.
*   **Digitalization**: Minimizing paper usage and optimizing processes.
*   **Cloud Services**: Using data centers that run on clean energy.
*   **Hardware Policies**: Fomenting the purchase of sustainable hardware and implementing recycling programs.
