## About Us
### Who We Are
23people is a technology services company dedicated to delivering high-impact software development solutions. We partner with clients to tackle significant challenges, building cohesive teams and implementing proven methodologies to create sustainable value.

### What We Do
We specialize in providing **agile software development** services to solve complex business needs. Our core service offerings include forming and scaling high-performance agile development teams, modernizing critical technology platforms without service disruption, and rapidly delivering custom applications. Examples of our work include developing a telemedicine scheduling platform to ensure patient care continuity during a crisis and updating a key credit reporting system for a major financial services client.

### What Sets Us Apart
Our primary differentiator is our focus on building **sustainable development capabilities** for our clients. We go beyond simple staff augmentation by creating cohesive, deeply integrated teams that operate as strategic extensions of our clients' organizations. Our approach combines a flexible recruitment strategy with structured acceleration programs, a strong commitment to **technical excellence**, and a culture of **continuous improvement**. This allows us to reduce hiring risks for our clients, accelerate their digital transformation, and deliver complex projects on time and on budget.
